🎯 Our Mission

FinCalc India was built with a simple belief — every Indian deserves access to accurate, free financial tools. Whether you're calculating your first home loan EMI, planning SIP investments, or figuring out your income tax — you shouldn't need expensive software or a CA consultation for basic calculations.

We built 13+ calculators covering EMI, SIP, Income Tax, GST, FD, RD, Retirement Planning, Inflation, Loan Eligibility, and more. Every calculator is free, requires no signup, works offline, and gives instant results with beautiful charts.

⚡ What Makes Us Different

  • 100% Free, No Signup Required
  • Works Offline (PWA)
  • Made for Indian Tax Laws & Financial System
  • Accurate Formulas with Transparent Calculations
  • Beautiful Charts & PDF Export
  • Regular Updates with Latest Tax Slabs & Rates
  • Open Source (MIT License)
