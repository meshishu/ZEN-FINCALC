📱 4. iPhone aur US Products — Price Change?

Agar India-US trade deal hota hai aur Apple India mein manufacturing badhata hai, toh iPhone aur US tech products India mein 5-10% saste ho sakte hain. Apple already shift kar raha hai — iPhone 16 India mein assemble ho rahi hai. Trade deal se incentive aur badh jayega.

💡 Apple India Opportunity: India mein Apple manufacturing = lakho direct + indirect jobs. Tamil Nadu aur Karnataka mein Foxconn/Tata Electronics ke plants. Trade deal se duty structure improve hone se Apple ki India manufacturing economics improve hongi. Yeh actually India ke liye net positive hai.

❓ FAQ — India-US Trade Deal 2026

India-US trade deal kab tak hoga?
July 2026 tak preliminary bilateral trade deal possible hai — PM Modi ne US visit ki planning ki hai aur negotiations active hain. 90-day tariff pause ke dauran dono countries aggressive diplomacy kar rahe hain. Full deal 12-18 months mein complete hoga, lekin preliminary framework July tak expected hai.
Trade deal hone se common man ko kya fayda hoga?
Direct benefits: (1) iPhone aur US tech products India mein sasta ho sakta hai — agar Apple India mein manufacture barhata hai toh. (2) IT jobs: TCS/Infosys/Wipro ke US contracts safer. (3) Rupee stabilize ho sakta hai — currently ₹94/$ pe. (4) Garment/textile workers ke jobs safe honge. Indirect: economic growth se overall jobs better.
Trade deal nahi hua toh kya?
26% tariff permanent ho jayega. IT exports pe indirect pressure. Garment sector mein layoffs possible — Bangladesh/Vietnam India se zyada competitive ho jayenge. Rupee further weak ho sakta hai ₹96-98 tak. Inflation 0.5-1% extra badh sakti hai. Overall GDP growth 0.3-0.5% lower.
Apple India mein zyada manufacturing karega?
Haan — Apple already India mein major production shift kar raha hai. iPhone 16 ab India mein assemble ho rahi hai. Trade deal se incentive milega Apple ko India mein base badhane ka. Yeh actually India ke liye bada opportunity hai — lakho direct/indirect jobs create honge.
Common man apni finance kaisa plan kare?
Short term (3-6 months): emergency fund strong rakho, SIP jari rakho, zyada risk mat lo. Medium term (6-12 months): agar deal hota hai toh IT/export stocks recover karenge — SIP le benefit. Long term: India ka fundamentals strong hai — demographics, domestic consumption, services growth. Systematic investment beats market timing.
