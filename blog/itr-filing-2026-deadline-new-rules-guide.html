📋 1. Pehle Samajhein — Yeh Saal ITR Kya Special Hai?

FY 2025-26 (April 2025 – March 2026) ka income tax return — yani AY 2026-27 — is saal ek transition year hai. India mein pehli baar New Income Tax Act 2025 officially implement hua hai jo decades purani Income Tax Act 1961 ki jagah le raha hai. Yeh act abhi poori tarah se enforce nahi hua — lekin form names aur numbers badal gaye hain aur kuch sections rewritten ho gaye hain.

Doosra bada change yeh hai ki New Tax Regime ab default regime hai. Matlab agar aapne kuch nahi kiya — toh portal assume karega ki aap New Regime mein hain. Old regime ke liye specifically opt-in karna padega. Salaried taxpayers ke liye yeh har saal ITR time pe switch kar sakte hain.

📊 2026 ITR Season Ke 3 Sabse Bade Changes:
1. Form numbers rename hue — Form 16 → Form 130, Form 26AS → Form 168, Form 12BB → Form 124
2. New Regime default ban gayi — ₹75,000 standard deduction milti hai, koi aur deduction nahi
3. Business taxpayers ki extended deadline — non-audit business income waale ab August 31 tak file kar sakte hain (pehle July 31 tha)

Teen saal mein ITR filers 28% badhein hain — 2022-23 ke 6.77 crore se ab 2025-26 mein expected 8.5 crore+. Tax base badh raha hai aur Income Tax Department ka processing system bhi upgrade hua hai. Refund processing pehle se fast hai — average 15-45 din mein refund aa jaata hai (pehle 90-180 din lagte the).

⚠️ Dhyan Rakhein: Aksar log samajhte hain ki employer ne TDS kaat liya toh ITR bharna zaruri nahi. Yeh galat hai. Agar income ₹2.5 lakh se zyada hai, ITR bharna mandatory hai — chahe TDS zero bhi ho. Plus ITR refund, loan applications, visa, aur future carry-forward losses ke liye zaroori document hai.

🔢 6. Step-by-Step ITR-1 Filing Guide — Salaried Employees Ke Liye

Agar aap salaried hain, single employer hai, income ₹50L se kam hai aur capital gains nahi hain — ITR-1 (Sahaj) aapka form hai. Yeh process follow karo:

Step 1 — Portal Login
incometax.gov.in pe jaao → e-File → Income Tax Returns → File Income Tax Return. PAN se login karo. Aadhar linked hona chahiye.
Step 2 — Assessment Year Select
AY 2026-27 select karo (FY 2025-26 ka return). ITR-1 form choose karo. New Regime ya Old Regime select karo — portal default New Regime dikhayega.
Step 3 — Personal Info + Bank
Personal details verify karo. Bank account pre-validate karein (agar nahi kiya toh 'Pre-validate Bank Account' option hai). Refund isi account mein aayega.
Step 4 — Pre-filled Data Check
Portal Form 26AS se salary aur TDS data automatically pre-fill karta hai. Har entry ko Form 16 / Form 130 se verify karo. Discrepancy mili toh correct karo.
Step 5 — Deductions Add Karo
Old Regime mein 80C, 80D, HRA, home loan interest enter karo. New Regime mein kuch nahi — sirf ₹75,000 standard deduction automatic milti hai.
Step 6 — Tax Computation Review
Portal automatically tax calculate karega. Total tax payable — TDS = Refund ya balance tax. Agar balance tax hai toh challan 280 se pay karo BEFORE submitting ITR.
Step 7 — Submit + Verify
ITR submit karo. Acknowledgement number milega (ITR-V). 30 din ke andar verify karo — Aadhar OTP ya Net Banking EVC se. Yahi final step hai.

❌ 7. Common Mistakes Jo Reject Karwate Hain

Yeh 7 mistakes defective return notice ya processing delay ka sabse bada reason hain. Inhe avoid karo:

  • 🔴 Wrong ITR form select karna — capital gains the toh ITR-2 chahiye tha, ITR-1 bhara — defective return
  • 🔴 AIS/26AS se income mismatch — portal ka pre-fill aur aapki declaration alag hai — notice aata hai
  • 🔴 Bank account pre-validate nahi kiya — refund return ho jaata hai
  • 🔴 ITR verify nahi kiya 30 din mein — return invalid ho jaata hai, dobara file karna padta hai
  • 🔴 Interest income chupa diya — FD interest, savings account interest AIS mein clearly show hota hai. Department match karta hai
  • 🔴 Employer name mismatch — TAN number galat ya company name alag — TDS mismatch notice
  • 🔴 Previous year losses carry forward bhool gaye — agar stock loss tha jo carry forward karna tha — ITR-2 zaroori tha, ITR-1 se nahi hoga
🚨 Sabse Dangerous Mistake: AIS mein koi income show ho rahi hai jo aapko pata nahi — jaise kisi purani FD ka maturity, ya stock dividend — aur aapne declare nahi kiya. Department automatically match karta hai aur notice + penalty dono aa sakte hain. AIS zaroor check karo submission se pehle.

🔁 8. ITR-U (Updated Return) — Galti Ho Gayi Toh Kya Karein

FY 2025-26 ka ITR file karne ke baad agar aapko koi galti mili — income bhaali, form galat tha, ya kuch deduction choot gayi — toh teen options hain:

Option A: Revised Return (u/s 139(5))

Deadline: July 31, 2026 ya March 31, 2027 (jo pehle aaye) tak file kar sakte ho. No extra tax — sirf correct information daalo. Portal pe "Revised" select karo aur original acknowledgement number daalo. Yeh sabse easy aur preferred option hai.

Option B: ITR-U (Updated Return, u/s 139(8A))

Agar assessment year khatam ho gayi ya deadline miss ho gayi. ITR-U ke through 2 assessment years peeche tak return update kar sakte ho. Lekin ek catch hai — extra tax dena padega:

  • 1st year mein file karo (AY 2024-25 ka update till March 2026): 25% extra tax on additional tax due
  • 2nd year mein file karo (AY 2024-25 ka update till March 2027): 50% extra tax on additional tax due
✅ Practical Advice: Agar sirf minor details galat hain (spelling mistake, address etc.) — revised return hi bharo. Agar income underreported kiya tha aur department notice aane se pehle correct karna chahte ho — ITR-U file karo, penalty se bachne ke liye better hai. Notice ke baad correct karna zyada expensive hota hai.

    ❓ 10. Frequently Asked Questions — ITR 2026

    ITR-1 aur ITR-2 mein kya fark hai?
    ITR-1 (Sahaj) un resident individuals ke liye hai jinki income sirf salary, ek ghar ki property aur interest se ho — aur total income ₹50 lakh se kam ho, ek hi employer ho. ITR-2 un logon ke liye hai jinke paas capital gains hain (stocks, mutual funds, property sale), ya 2 se zyada ghar hain, ya foreign income/assets hain. Agar ek bhi mutual fund redemption ya stock sell kiya hai saal mein — ITR-2 use karo, ITR-1 nahi.
    July 31 se pehle file karna zaruri hai kya agar refund expect karta hun?
    Haan, refund jaldi pane ke liye July 31 ki deadline zarur pakad lo. Technically December 31 tak belated return file ho sakta hai lekin ₹5,000 late fee lagti hai (income ₹5L se kam ho toh ₹1,000). Belated return mein refund processing bhi late start hoti hai — December mein file karo toh refund March-April 2027 tak aa sakta hai. July 31 se pehle file + verify karo toh September-October 2026 mein refund expect karo.
    Form 16 employer ne nahi diya — kya karein?
    Agar employer ne Form 16 (ab Form 130) nahi diya hai toh Form 26AS ya AIS (Annual Information Statement) use karein — incometax.gov.in → Login → e-File → View AIS. Wahan TDS section mein apni salary TDS clearly dikhega. HR ko written email bhejo — June 15 employer ki legal deadline hai Form 16 dene ki. Agar phir bhi nahi mila toh salary slips se manually total salary calculate karo aur AIS TDS figures use karo. Employer se Form 16 nahi milna Income Tax Act violation hai — complaint kar sakte ho.
    New regime choose kiya hai toh ITR mein kya alag hoga?
    New regime mein ITR form (ITR-1 ya ITR-2) same rahega lekin Part B-TTI mein regime selection clearly mark karni hogi — "New Tax Regime (115BAC)" select karo. New regime mein koi deduction (80C, 80D, HRA, home loan interest etc.) claim nahi hogi — portal automatically deduction fields grey out kar deta hai. Sirf ₹75,000 standard deduction automatically milti hai. Tax slabs new regime ke hisaab se calculate honge — ₹12L tak practically zero tax (₹87A rebate + standard deduction).
    Capital gains hain mutual fund se — kaunsa form bharein?
    Mutual fund ya stocks se capital gains hain toh ITR-2 bharein — ITR-1 se nahi hoga. ITR portal mein CAMS ya KFin consolidated capital gains statement upload karo — portal automatically import kar leta hai. Short-term capital gains (STCG within 1 year for equity — 20% tax from 2024 budget changes), long-term capital gains (LTCG above 1 year equity — ₹1.25 lakh exempt, upar 12.5% tax). Broker se bhi annual P&L statement download karo.
    ITR verify kaise karein — Aadhar OTP, NetBanking, ya kuch aur?
    ITR file karne ke 30 din ke andar verify karna mandatory hai warna return invalid ho jaata hai. Verification ke main options: (1) Aadhar OTP — sabse fast, Aadhar registered mobile pe OTP aata hai, 2-minute kaam. (2) Net Banking EVC — SBI, HDFC, ICICI, IDBI etc. ke net banking se portal pe EVC generate karo. (3) DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) — professionals/companies ke liye. (4) Bank ATM EVC — select banks. (5) Physical ITR-V — print karke CPC Bangalore bhejo — 10-15 din process hota hai (last resort).
    Galat form bhar diya — kya ab sahi kar sakte hain?
    Haan! Revised return (u/s 139(5)) file karo. July 31, 2026 deadline se pehle ya assessment year khatam hone se pehle (March 31, 2027) file kar sakte ho — koi extra fee nahi. Portal pe: Login → e-File → Income Tax Returns → File Income Tax Return → "Revised" select karo → original acknowledgement number enter karo → sab correct karo → submit + verify. Ek baar revised return file karne ke baad original automatically supersede ho jaati hai.
    Refund kitne din mein aata hai normally 2026 mein?
    ITR file + verify ke baad normal processing: 15–45 din. Fast cases mein 7–10 din bhi aa jaata hai agar bank account pre-validated hai aur koi mismatch nahi. Delay ke main reasons: bank account validation pending, AIS mismatch, defective return notice. Refund status check: incometax.gov.in login → e-File → ITR Status. Ya NSDL refund tracking portal pe PAN + AY enter karo. Refund bounce hona (account mismatch) zyada common problem hai — bank account zaroor pre-validate karo.
