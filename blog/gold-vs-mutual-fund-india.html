🥇 Gold vs Mutual Fund — Seedhi Baat

India mein gold sirf ek investment nahi — yeh ek emotion hai, ek tradition hai. Lekin jab baat real wealth creation ki aati hai, to data ko sunna padta hai. Kya gold ne past 20 saalon mein equity mutual funds ko beat kiya? Kya gold sirf crisis mein kaam aata hai? NRI ke liye kaun sa option better hai?

Is guide mein hum 20 saal ka verified return data, SIP simulation, tax comparison (Budget 2024 updated), NRI rules aur expert verdict — sab kuch cover karenge. No jargon, only actionable insights.

⚡ Quick Verdict: Long-term (15-20 saal) ke liye Equity Mutual Fund SIP historically better returns deta hai (~12-15% CAGR). Gold ka role portfolio diversification, hedge aur stability hai — pure returns ke liye SIP wins. Ideal mix: 85% equity SIP + 15% gold.

🌍 NRI Investment Guide — Gold vs Mutual Fund

NRI investors ke liye dono options available hain, lekin kuch important differences hain:

NRI Gold Investment Options:

  • Physical Gold: India visit pe khareed sakte ho, customs limit ke andar
  • Gold ETF: NRI demat account (NRE/NRO) se invest kar sakte hain
  • Gold Mutual Funds: NRI invest kar sakte hain (KYC required)
  • SGB: NRI eligibility issuer guidelines aur RBI rules ke hisaab se apply hoti hai — current status verify karein

NRI Mutual Fund Investment:

  • NRE/NRO account se invest kar sakte hain
  • FATCA/CRS compliance required
  • US/Canada NRI ke liye kuch fund houses restrictions apply kar sakte hain
  • Repatriation: NRE account se freely repatriable
  • TDS applicable on gains
ℹ️ NRI Note: Tax treaty benefits aur exact repatriation rules aapke resident country pe depend karte hain. FEMA guidelines aur double taxation avoidance agreement (DTAA) dhyan mein rakhein. Qualified financial advisor se consult karo before investing.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Gold ya Mutual Fund — kaun better investment hai India mein?
Long-term (15-20 saal) ke liye Equity Mutual Fund (SIP) historically better returns deta hai — ~12-15% CAGR vs gold ke ~8-10% CAGR. Lekin gold portfolio diversification aur crisis hedge ke liye important hai. Ideal allocation: 10-15% gold, baaki equity SIP.
Gold pe kitna tax lagta hai India mein 2026?
Physical gold aur Gold ETF: 24 months se zyada hold pe 12.5% LTCG (no indexation) Budget 2024 ke baad. 24 months se kam: income tax slab rate pe STCG. SGB (Sovereign Gold Bond) maturity pe completely tax-free. Equity Mutual Fund: 12 months se zyada pe 12.5% LTCG above ₹1.25L/year.
NRI gold invest kar sakte hain India mein?
Haan, NRI physical gold, Gold ETF aur Gold Mutual Fund mein invest kar sakte hain. SGB ke liye eligibility restrictions apply ho sakti hain — issuer guidelines aur RBI rules ke hisaab se check karein. Repatriation rules aur FEMA compliance bhi dhyan mein rakhein.
SIP aur Gold mein kitna invest karna chahiye?
Financial planners recommend: total portfolio ka 10-15% gold (physical/ETF/SGB), aur baaki 85-90% equity mutual fund SIP. Yeh allocation risk diversify karta hai aur long-term wealth creation bhi. Age ke saath equity allocation adjust karte raho.
Sovereign Gold Bond (SGB) vs Gold ETF — kaun better hai?
SGB: 2.5% annual interest + price appreciation + tax-free maturity (8 saal). Gold ETF: liquid, kisi bhi time sell karo, demat account mein hold, lekin LTCG tax lagta hai. Long-term ke liye SGB better; flexibility chahiye to Gold ETF.
