📋 1. Kya Hua Exactly? — Income Tax Act 1961 Replace Kyun Kiya?

India ka Income Tax Act 1961 — 64 saal purana tha. Uss time se abhi tak 6,000+ amendments ho chuki thi, language complex thi, aur 500 se zyada rules the jo aam aadmi samajh hi nahi sakta tha. Government ka target tha — isse simple banana, litigation reduce karna aur digital India ke liye ready karna.

Naye Income Tax Act 2025 ko Parliament mein 2025 mein pass kiya gaya, aur yeh April 1, 2026 se officially effective ho gaya. Iska major achievement hai — language ko simple English (aur effective Hinglish) mein likha gaya, rules ko reorganize kiya gaya, aur unnecessary provisions hata di gayi hain.

📊 Key Impact: Income Tax Act 1961 mein time ke saath itne amendments ho gaye the ki ek hi rule ke multiple interpretations possible the — isliye court cases bahut badhte ja rahe the. Naya act in contradictions ko resolve karta hai aur litigation reduce karne ka target hai.

Kya Purani ITR-filed Cases affected honge?

Nahi. Jo cases already pending hain ya jo July 2026 mein file ho rahe hain (AY 2026-27 ke liye) — woh sab purane Income Tax Act 1961 ke under hi process honge. Naya act sirf Tax Year 2026-27 (FY 2026-27) ki income se apply hoga.

📅 2. Tax Year Ka Naya Concept — FY aur AY Confusion Khatam

India mein pehle do terms tha jo logon ko confuse karta tha — Financial Year (FY) aur Assessment Year (AY). FY woh saal tha jismein income kamayi, aur AY woh saal tha jismein ITR file hota. Iska matlab tha ki FY 2025-26 ki income ke liye ITR AY 2026-27 mein file hota. Double confusion!

Naye act mein yeh simplify ho gaya. Ab sirf ek term hai — "Tax Year". Tax Year = Financial Year. Simple.

💡 Mapping Example:
Old terms: FY 2026-27 (income year) + AY 2027-28 (assessment year)
New term: Tax Year 2026-27 (ek hi term, wahi matlab)

Iska matlab: Jab bhi koi "Tax Year 2026-27" bole — samjho woh FY 2026-27 ki income ki baat kar raha hai.
July 31, 2026
Old Act ITR deadline — AY 2026-27 ka ITR file karo (FY 2025-26 ki income ke liye). Yeh purane Income Tax Act 1961 ke under file hoga. Salaried individuals ke liye July 31 hi deadline hai.
April 1, 2026
New Act Effective — Income Tax Act 2025 officially lagu. Tax Year 2026-27 ab is naye act ke under governed hoga. Salaried logon ke forms bhi gradually update honge.
July 31 / Aug 31, 2027
Pehla "New Act" ITR — Tax Year 2026-27 ka ITR. Yeh pehla return hoga jo naye Income Tax Act 2025 ke under file hoga. Naye forms, naye terminology.
⚠️ Common Mistake Mat Karna: Kai log soch rahe hain ki April 2026 mein file karna hai kuch naya. Aisa nahi hai. July 2026 mein jo ITR file hoga (FY 2025-26 ki income ke liye) woh purane act ke under hi hoga. Naya act tab apply hoga jab Tax Year 2026-27 ki income ka ITR 2027 mein file hoga.

💰 3. ₹12 Lakh Tax Free — Kya Yeh Badla? (Nahi Badla)

Yeh sabse common confusion hai. Log soch rahe hain naye act mein tax slabs change ho gaye. Bilkul nahi badla. Tax slabs same hain jo Budget 2025 mein announce hue the aur FY 2025-26 se effective hain.

New tax regime mein ₹12 lakh tak ka income effectively tax-free rehta hai rebate u/s 87A ki wajah se (maximum ₹60,000 rebate). Standard deduction ₹75,000 bhi same hai — matlab gross salary ₹12.75 lakh tak ka salaried person bhi effectively zero tax pays karta hai.

Note: Standard deduction ₹75,000 add karke — gross salary ₹12.75L ka ₹12L taxable income hota hai. Cess (4%) bhi applicable hota hai final tax pe.

✅ What Didn't Change: Tax slabs same hain. ₹12L ka rebate same hai. Standard deduction ₹75,000 same hai. New regime default same hai. Old regime bhi available hai HRA waalon ke liye. Naye act ka focus sirf simplification tha, tax rates change karna nahi.

🏙️ 4. HRA Exemption — 4 Nayi Cities Added (50% Category)

Yeh ek major practical change hai jo lakho IT professionals ko directly benefit karega. Pehle sirf 4 metros — Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai — 50% HRA exemption ke liye eligible the. Ab April 1, 2026 se 4 aur cities add ho gayi hain: Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad, aur Ahmedabad.

📌 Important Note: HRA exemption sirf Old Tax Regime mein milta hai. Agar aap new tax regime mein ho — jo ki default hai — toh HRA exempt nahi hoti chahe aap kisi bhi city mein raho. Yeh change specifically unke liye useful hai jo old regime choose karte hain.

HRA Calculation — Bangalore IT Professional Example

Ek Bangalore IT employee ki salary details:

  • Basic salary: ₹60,000/month
  • HRA received from employer: ₹30,000/month
  • Actual rent paid: ₹35,000/month (2BHK Bangalore)

👨‍💼 9. Salaried vs Business — Kisko Kya Impact?

Naya Income Tax Act 2025 sab pe equally impact nahi karta. Kuch groups ko zyada benefit hai, kuch ko zyada compliance change hai. Seedha samjhein:

🥧 New Act 2025 — Kisko Kitna Benefit?
Approximate benefit distribution across taxpayer segments

    Salaried Employees — Main Impact

    • HRA change: Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad residents ko 50% HRA exemption milega old regime mein — yeh biggest direct benefit hai
    • Form changes: Form 16 → Form 130. Employer aur IT portal dono pe update aayega — transition mein dono valid
    • Tax slabs: Same hain — ₹12L tak zero tax, standard deduction ₹75K same
    • ITR deadline: July 31 hi rahegi — no change
    • Vehicle perquisite: Company car waalon ka perquisite value thoda badha hai (₹8K/₹10K per month depending on engine)

    Business Owners & Professionals — Main Impact

    • ITR deadline: Non-audit cases ke liye July 31 → August 31 — extra mahina milta hai
    • Reassessment protection: Tax authority ko ab minimum 30 days ki notice deni hogi reassessment se pehle
    • F&O traders: STT slightly badhega options pe (0.1% vs 0.0625%)
    • TCS on overseas transfers: ₹7L se upar 20% TCS — foreign payment karte hain toh credit claim karna padega

    Startup Founders — Big Win

    Startup India scheme ke under registered startups ko pehle 3 consecutive saal mein 100% profit tax deduction milti thi — lekin condition thi ki turnover ₹100 crore se kam ho. Ab yeh limit badha ke ₹300 crore kar di gayi hai. Matlab zyada bade startups bhi eligible hain — jo Scale-up phase mein hain.

    NRIs — Key Updates

    • PAN rules same, Aadhaar-PAN link mandatory (NRI exemption bhi available)
    • TCS on LRS remittances: ₹7L se upar foreign transfer pe 20% TCS — claim on ITR karo
    • DTAA (Double Tax Avoidance Agreements) ke rules clarified — fewer disputes expected

    ❓ 10. FAQ — Aksar Pooche Jaane Waale Sawal

    Kya Income Tax Act 1961 khatam ho gaya?
    Income Tax Act 1961 ko Income Tax Act 2025 ne April 1, 2026 se replace kar diya hai. Lekin July 2026 mein file hone wala ITR (AY 2026-27, jo FY 2025-26 ki income ke liye hai) abhi bhi purane Income Tax Act 1961 ke under hi hoga. Naya act sirf Tax Year 2026-27 ki income pe apply hoga jiska ITR 2027 mein file hoga. Transition smooth rakhne ke liye pehle saal dono acts ke provisions cross-referencing bhi karni pad sakti hai.
    Kya mere tax slabs change ho gaye April 2026 se?
    Bilkul nahi. Tax slabs mein koi change nahi aaya hai naye Income Tax Act 2025 mein. New regime slabs same hain: 0-4L: 0%, 4-8L: 5%, 8-12L: 10%, 12-16L: 15%, 16-20L: 20%, 20-24L: 25%, 24L+: 30%. ₹12 lakh tak effectively tax-free rehega rebate u/s 87A ke through. Standard deduction ₹75,000 bhi same hai. Naye act ka focus sirf simplification tha — tax rates change karna nahi tha.
    Form 16 ab Form 130 ho gaya — kya employer naya form dega?
    Haan, technically Form 16 ka naam badal kar Form 130 ho gaya hai naye act mein. Lekin transition period mein dono names valid hain. Employers aur banks 2026-27 ke liye abhi bhi Form 16, 26AS, 15G terminology use kar sakte hain. Income Tax portal bhi dono names accept karega. Practically 2027-28 ke ITR cycle se fully new names use hone lagenge. Aapko kuch special karne ki zarurat nahi — jo form milega woh valid hoga.
    HRA exemption 50% Bangalore pe kab se milega?
    Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad aur Ahmedabad April 1, 2026 se 50% HRA exemption category mein aa gayi hain. Yeh benefit Tax Year 2026-27 (FY 2026-27, April 2026 – March 2027) ki income pe milega. Yaad rakhein — FY 2025-26 ki income (jiske liye July 2026 mein ITR file hoga) pe abhi bhi purane 40% rate wala rule apply hoga. Naye 50% rate ka pehla ITR 2027 mein file hoga.
    ITR deadline August 31 ho gaya — kya July 31 ki zarurat nahi?
    Salaried individuals ke liye ITR deadline abhi bhi July 31 hi hai — koi change nahi. August 31 ki extended deadline sirf non-audit business owners, self-employed professionals (doctors, lawyers, CAs, consultants), aur unke firm partners ke liye hai. Agar aap salaried employee hain toh July 31 se pehle hi ITR file karna hoga, warna penalty lagegi. Confusion social media pe spread ho raha hai — clarify kar lo.
    New tax regime vs old — kaunsa better hai 2026 mein?
    Ab Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad ya Ahmedabad mein rehne wale aur high rent pay karne wale IT professionals ke liye old tax regime aur attractive ho gayi hai — kyunki 50% HRA exemption ka significant tax benefit sirf old regime mein milta hai. New regime mein HRA exempt nahi hoti. Jo log large HRA exemption claim kar sakte hain (rent ₹30K+ per month, high basic salary) — unke liye old regime check karna chahiye. Calculator use karo aur compare karo — FinCalc India pe free tool hai.
    Startup founder hoon — ₹300 crore threshold kya matlab?
    Startup India scheme ke under registered startups ko incorporation se 10 saal ke andar 3 consecutive years mein 100% profit tax deduction milti hai. Pehle yeh benefit sirf tab milta tha jab startup ka annual turnover ₹100 crore se kam ho. Naye act mein yeh threshold badha kar ₹300 crore kar di gayi hai. Matlab ₹100 crore se ₹300 crore turnover wale growing startups bhi ab eligible hain is massive tax benefit ke liye — yeh scaling startups ke liye bada fayda hai.
    PAN card nahi hai, kyun zaruri hua?
    Naye act mein PAN mandatory transactions ki thresholds update ho gayi hain — specifically jewellery ka threshold ₹5 lakh se ghata kar ₹2 lakh ho gaya hai. Bina PAN ke aap ₹2 lakh se zyada ki jewellery legally nahi kharid sakte (jeweller pan verify karega). Aadhaar-PAN link mandatory hai — agar link nahi hai toh TDS higher rate pe katega. Agar abhi tak PAN nahi liya hai toh NSDL ya UTIITSL portal se apply karo — 10-15 working days mein milta hai.
