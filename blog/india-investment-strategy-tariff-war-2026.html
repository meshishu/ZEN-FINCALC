📊 1. Tariff War Investment Reality Check

Har global crisis mein 2 type ke investors hote hain: (1) Panic sellers — jo crisis mein sab bech dete hain aur recovery miss karte hain. (2) Opportunity buyers — jo systematic rehte hain ya crash mein zyada invest karte hain. Historical data kahin zyada clear hai: 2008 crash ke baad next 5 years mein Nifty +280% tha.

📈 Historical Data Point: 2020 COVID crash mein Nifty -38% hua. Jo investors SIP jari rakhe ya extra invest kiya, unka 2-year return 65%+ tha. Tariff war 2026 — short-term noise hai, long-term India story intact hai.

🏦 3. Defensive Stocks — Kaun Safe Hai?

Tariff war mein domestic-focused companies relatively better perform karti hain. Export revenue zyada hone wali companies pe direct pressure hota hai.

✅ Safe / Defensive Sectors
  • FMCG — HUL, ITC, Britannia, Dabur (domestic demand)
  • Banking/NBFC — SBI, HDFC Bank (domestic credit)
  • Infrastructure — L&T, NBCC (govt capex)
  • Healthcare Domestic — Cipla, Sun Pharma domestic
⚠️ Risky / Avoid Now
  • IT exporters — TCS, Infosys, Wipro (US revenue hit)
  • Textile exporters — US tariff direct hit
  • Metals — global commodity price pressure
  • Small/mid cap growth stocks (high beta)

🏦 6. Emergency Fund — First Priority

Kisi bhi investment strategy se pehle: 3-6 months ka emergency fund liquid account ya FD mein rakho. Tariff war mein job uncertainty ho sakti hai (garment, IT sector). Emergency fund hone se tum market correction mein bhi SIP continue kar sakte ho bina portfolio tode.

⚠️ Priority Order: (1) Emergency fund complete karo — 3-6 months expenses. (2) High-interest debt clear karo. (3) SIP jari rakho / shuru karo. (4) Gold ETF/SGB 10-15%. (5) Short-term FD for stability. Order matter karta hai!

❓ FAQ — Investment Strategy Tariff War

Tariff war mein SIP band karni chahiye?
Bilkul nahi — SIP jari rakhni chahiye. Market correction mein SIP ke through zyada units milte hain kam price pe. Historical data: 2008 crash mein bhi jo SIP jari rakhe unko next 5 years mein 22% CAGR mila. Panic selling sabse bada mistake hai.
Tariff war mein gold kitna badhega?
Gold already 2026 mein ₹1,48,000 per 10gm pe hai — 1 saal mein 54% return. Safe haven demand tariff uncertainty mein aur badhti hai. Global analysts ₹1,60,000-₹1,75,000 range April-June 2026 mein predict kar rahe hain. 5-10% portfolio gold exposure reasonable hai.
Kaunse domestic stocks safe hain tariff war mein?
Domestic consumption focused companies: FMCG (HUL, ITC, Britannia), pharma domestic sales (Cipla, Dr Reddy domestic segment), banking (SBI, HDFC Bank), infrastructure (L&T, NBCC) — in companies ka revenue mostly domestic hai, export ke US tariff se directly impact kam hai.
FD safe hai tariff war mein?
Haan — short-term FD (1-2 year) ek smart move hai. SBI/HDFC currently 6.5-7.0% FD rates de rahe hain. Tariff uncertainty mein liquid rahna important hai. Deposits up to ₹5L insured hain DICGC se. 3-6 months ki emergency fund zaroor maintain karo.
Kya dollar-denominated investment karni chahiye?
NRIs ke liye: FCNR/NRE accounts attractive hain weak rupee mein. Resident Indians ke liye: international mutual funds (US/global ETFs) via liberalized remittance scheme (LRS $250K/year). Lekin pehle domestic emergency fund complete karo phir international diversification sochna.
