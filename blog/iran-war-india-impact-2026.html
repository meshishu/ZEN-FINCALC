🔴 1. Kya Hua? — Iran War Timeline

28 February 2026 ko US aur Israel ne surprise airstrikes se Iran pe attack kiya. Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei ki hatya hui, multiple military leaders mare gaye, aur Iran ne jawab me 500+ ballistic missiles aur 2,000 drones launch kiye. Aaj Day 33 hai — yeh conflict ab full-scale war me badal chuka hai.

28 Feb 2026
War shuru: US-Israel surprise airstrikes. Supreme Leader Khamenei killed. Iran retaliates with missiles.
1-4 Mar 2026
Strait of Hormuz band: Iran ne 20% global oil supply route block kiya. Crude oil prices double.
5-13 Mar 2026
Oil shock intensifies: Brent crude crosses $120/barrel. India gets emergency waiver for Russian oil.
20-27 Mar 2026
India's response: Govt cuts excise duty ₹10/litre. Rupee crosses 93.94. Stock market crashes 10%.
1-2 Apr 2026
Day 33: Trump says war to end in 2-3 weeks. Shell hikes premium petrol ₹7.41. ATF up 8.5%. Peace talks uncertain.
⚠️ Key Fact: IEA (International Energy Agency) ke according, Strait of Hormuz ka band hona "history ka sabse bada oil supply disruption" hai. Duniya ka 20% oil (20 million barrels/day) yahan se guzarta tha.

🌊 2. Strait of Hormuz — India Ka Lifeline

Strait of Hormuz Persian Gulf me 60 km choda samudri raasta hai. India ke liye yeh isliye critical hai kyunki hum apna 40% crude oil aur 80% natural gas Gulf countries (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Iraq, Kuwait, Qatar) se import karte hain. Jab se 4 March ko Iran ne yeh route effectively block kiya hai, India ki energy supply chain collapse ho rhi hai.

🌍 9. NRI & Gulf Workers — Massive Crisis

March 2026 tak 2,20,000+ Indian nationals Gulf countries se vapas laaye gaye hain. Kuwait airport band hai, Dubai me real estate buyers disappear ho gaye, aur remittance flow pe bhi pressure hai.

📊 NRI Numbers:
• 2.2 Lakh+ Indians repatriated from Gulf (March 2026)
• 70,000+ Afghan refugees returned (forced, under war pressure)
• India's 30% remittances come from Middle East = 1%+ of GDP
• Gulf "Returnee" capital flowing into Tier-2/Tier-3 city real estate (+14% growth)

✅ 10. Aam Aadmi Kya Kare? — 7 Smart Money Moves

1. 🛢️ Fuel Budget Badhao: Monthly fuel budget 20-30% extra rakhlo. Possible hai petrol ₹110+ jaaye coming months me.
2. 💰 Emergency Fund = 6 Months: Agar 3 months ka hai toh 6 months tak badhao. Job loss risk sectors: aviation, hospitality, oil marketing.
3. 📈 SIP Band Mat Karo: Market crash = cheap buying opportunity. History shows war-time dips 6-12 months me recover hote hain. SIP continue rakhna long-term wealth ke liye best.
4. 🥇 Gold 10-15% Portfolio Me Rakhlo: Gold ₹13,880+/gram hai — crisis me aur badh sakta hai. SGB (Sovereign Gold Bond) sabse tax-efficient option.

5. 🏦 FD Lock Karo 1-2 Year: Rates 7-8% pe hain — agar RBI rate cut karta hai toh FD rates girengi. Abhi lock karna smart hai.
6. 🏠 Home Loan Prepay Karo: Agar spare cash hai toh partial prepayment karo — rate hike se pehle principal kam karo. EMI load reduce hoga.
7. 📊 Portfolio Rebalance: IT, Pharma, Defence stocks relatively safe hain. Oil marketing, airlines, auto se exposure kam karo temporarily.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Iran war se India me petrol kitna mehnga hoga?
Abhi petrol Delhi me ₹94.77 aur Mumbai me ₹103.54 hai. Government ne excise duty ₹10/litre cut ki hai. Agar crude $120+ rehta hai toh petrol ₹110-120 possible. Shell ne already premium petrol Bengaluru me ₹119.85 kiya hai.
Iran war se rupee kitna gir sakta hai?
Record low 93.94 touch ho chuka hai. RBI $12-15 billion defend me laga chuka hai. Worst case (oil $120+) me rupee 97.50 possible. 100/USD unlikely but possible agar war 3+ months chale.
Iran war me kahan invest karna chahiye?
Gold safest — already ₹14,000+/gram. FD rates stable 7-8%. SIP continue karo long-term ke liye. Defence, pharma, FMCG stocks relatively safe. Oil marketing companies avoid karo.
Strait of Hormuz kya hai aur India ko kaise affect karta hai?
Strait of Hormuz Persian Gulf ka 60km choda raasta hai jahan se duniya ka 20% oil guzarta hai. India 40% crude oil aur 80% gas Gulf se import karta hai. Feb 28 se yeh band hai, isliye oil 60%+ badha hai.
Home loan EMI badhegi kya Iran war ki wajah se?
RBI ne rates hold kiye hain. Agar inflation 6%+ cross karta hai toh 0.25-0.50% hike possible. ₹50 lakh loan pe EMI ₹800-1,600/month badh sakti hai. Abhi prepayment karna smart hai.
