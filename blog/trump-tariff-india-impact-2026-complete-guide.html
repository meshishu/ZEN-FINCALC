🌍 1. US Tariff Kya Hai? India Ko Kyun Laga?

US President Donald Trump ne April 2, 2026 ko "Liberation Day" declare kiya aur multiple countries pe reciprocal tariffs announce kiye. India ko 26% tariff laga — US ka logic yeh tha ki India US goods pe average 12% tariff lagata hai (US claim: "26%"), isliye US bhi 26% lagayega.

💵 4. Rupee Aur Inflation Pe Impact

Rupee April 2026 mein 94.2 per USD pe aa gaya — record low. Weak rupee matlab: imported items (crude oil, electronics, raw materials) mehenge hote hain, inflation badhti hai. RBI intervention possible hai lekin limited reserves ka pressure bhi rahega.

⚠️ Inflation Watch: Rupee 94 pe = crude oil import cost +15%. Petrol price ke baad transport costs badhenge. Core inflation 0.5-0.8% extra expected. Common man ke liye: grocery, transport, electronics sab thoda mehenge honge.

❓ FAQ — Trump Tariff India Impact 2026

Trump tariff India pe kitna laga?
US ne India pe 26% reciprocal tariff April 2, 2026 ko announce kiya. Pehle India pe average 13.5% US tariff tha, ab 26% ho gaya — yani almost double. China pe 145%, Bangladesh pe 37%, Vietnam pe 46% — India relatively better position mein hai.
Kaun se sectors sabse zyada affected hain?
IT/Software exports: technically tariffs directly apply nahi hote services pe, lekin indirect impact — US clients apna spend cut kar sakte hain. Pharma: $8B exports at risk — generic drugs pe scrutiny. Textile/Garments: direct competition Bangladesh, Vietnam se. Auto components: $2B affected. Gems & Jewellery: $9B exports pe impact.
Indian stock market pe kya asar hua?
Tariff announcement ke baad Sensex ek haftein mein -8% gira. IT stocks (TCS, Infosys, Wipro) -10-15% gire kyunki US clients apna IT budget cut kar sakte hain. Pharma stocks mixed — Dr Reddy's, Sun Pharma niche aaye. Defense stocks aur domestic-focused companies relatively safe rahe.
Kya India-US trade deal hoga?
India aur US dono trade deal pe actively negotiate kar rahe hain — July 2026 tak preliminary deal possible hai. Modi government 90-day pause ke dauran aggressively negotiate kar rahi hai. Agar deal hua toh tariffs 26% se kam ho sakte hain. Dekhte hain.
Common man pe kya asar hoga?
Petrol, LPG pe directly koi asar nahi. Lekin rupee kamzor hoga toh inflation badh sakti hai. Import pe depend items — electronics, raw materials mehenge honge. Jobs: export-oriented garment factories mein layoffs possible. Overall: 6-12 months mein slow GDP growth expected.
